❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I activate my license?

Go to License menu → Activate License, copy your Machine ID, and email it to fera2007@gmail.com with your license type. You'll receive a JSON file to import within 24 hours.

Why isn't YouTube download working?

Make sure yt-dlp.exe is in the program folder. You can download it manually from yt-dlp releases. Also check your internet connection and firewall settings.

Torrent downloads not starting?

Ensure aria2c.exe is present (64-bit or 32-bit version matching your system). Check your firewall settings and try adding trackers manually.

Can I use the program in Arabic?

Yes! Fera fully supports both Arabic (RTL) and English. Switch languages instantly from the Language menu.

How do I report a bug?

Open an issue on GitHub Issues with detailed steps, screenshots, and your system information.

What's the refund policy?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all plans (60 days for Lifetime). Contact support for assistance.

Can I transfer my license to another computer?

Yes! You can deactivate your license from the old computer and activate on the new one. Contact support if you need assistance.
